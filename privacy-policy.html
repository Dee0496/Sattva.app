Privacy Policy
Effective date: 28 April 2026
Sattva is a Vedic yoga and wellness app that guides you through breathing exercises, meditation, and relaxation practices. Your privacy matters to us — this policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account & payment data: If you purchase a subscription, payment is processed by Google Play or Stripe. We receive a transaction confirmation and your email address, but we do not store your credit card or billing details directly.

Usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated information about which sessions you use and how often, solely to improve the app experience. This data cannot identify you personally.

Device information: Basic device metadata (operating system version, screen size) may be collected for compatibility and debugging purposes.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Sattva does not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Your precise or approximate location
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Microphone or camera data
  • Health or fitness data beyond your in-app session selections
  • Personal identifiers for advertising purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

Any data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver and maintain the app's guided sessions and features
  • Process subscription purchases and verify entitlements
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance
  • Communicate important service updates to subscribers

4. Third-Party Services

Sattva integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Billing — for subscription management on Android
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Supabase — for backend data storage (hosted in Singapore)

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Supabase (Singapore region) and Railway, with HTTPS enforced for all communications. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

6. Children's Privacy

Sattva is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you at any time by contacting us. If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under the GDPR including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
